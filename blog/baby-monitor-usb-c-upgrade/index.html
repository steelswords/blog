Upgrading my VTech Baby Monitor

There is not much to tell here, but more to show. Our baby monitor works fine. It's great. The battery is perhaps a little weedy, but what can you expect from NiMH? My only irritation with it was the barrel plug its charger used. It is super inconvenient to be tied to one baby monitor charger when we have all these perfectly good USB-C chargers all over the house. It bugged me that I couldn't plug the monitor into USB-C, The Universal Chargerâ„¢, so I fixed it.

The DC power supply the monitor came with has 6V at a 400 mA.
